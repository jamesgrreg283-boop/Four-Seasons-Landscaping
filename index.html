FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our landscaping services in Coventry.

How much does landscaping cost in Coventry?

Landscaping costs vary depending on the scope of your project. A small patio may start from £3,000, while full garden transformations can range from £8,000 to £30,000+. We provide free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your specific requirements and budget.

How long do garden transformations take?

Project timelines depend on complexity. A patio installation typically takes 3–5 days, artificial grass 1–2 days, and full garden transformations 2–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during your free consultation.

Do you provide free quotations?

Yes — we offer completely free, no-obligation quotations. We'll visit your property, discuss your vision and provide a detailed, transparent quote with no hidden costs.

Do you install artificial grass in Coventry?

Absolutely. We're specialists in premium artificial grass installation across Coventry and surrounding areas. We use top-grade turf with full ground preparation, natural drainage and expert finishing for a flawless, year-round lawn.

Can you install patios and driveways?

Yes — patios, driveways and block paving are core services. We install porcelain patios, natural stone, block paved driveways and resin surfaces with premium materials and expert craftsmanship.

Which areas do you cover?

We cover Coventry, Nuneaton, Bedworth, Rugby, Kenilworth, Leamington Spa, Warwick and all surrounding areas across the West Midlands. Contact us to confirm we cover your location.
